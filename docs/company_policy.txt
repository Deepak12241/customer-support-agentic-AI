ABC TECHNOLOGIES - COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT

1. REFUND POLICY
- Refund requests are allowed only for annual subscriptions within 30 days of the purchase date.
- All refund requests must be escalated to a human supervisor for review and approval before processing.
- Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable.

2. SUBSCRIPTION CANCELLATION
- Customers may cancel their subscription at any time.
- Cancellation requests for active enterprise contracts must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor to check for contractual obligations.
- Standard cancellation requests are routed to Account/Billing but must undergo supervisor validation to offer retention plans.

3. ACCOUNT CLOSURE
- Account closure requests are permanent and will result in the deletion of all customer data.
- To prevent accidental deletion, all account closure requests must be reviewed and approved by a human supervisor.

4. COMPENSATION POLICY
- In cases of system outages or verified service issues, support agents can offer a maximum compensation of a $10 billing credit or a 5-day subscription extension.
- Any compensation offer must be reviewed and approved by a supervisor before being communicated to the customer.

5. ESCALATION TO MANAGEMENT
- If a customer is highly unsatisfied and demands escalation to executive management, the request must be approved by a supervisor.
- Support agents cannot directly forward issues to management without supervisor validation.
